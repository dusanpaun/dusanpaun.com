GITHUB

“Fool me once, shame on you, fool me twice, shame on me.”

I believe that everyone deserves a second chance, Microsoft is doing great things in open-source now. But I remember how their view on this world was when Bill wanted to eradicate this "pandemic" of free stuff.

Microsoft did not create GitHub, he only did acquire it, but in 10 to 15 years I do not want to see another CentOS fiasco.

And for that reason, I will lawfully stick with GitLab and gladly wait for you on the other side when you understand that privacy is not a feature but a requirement.
